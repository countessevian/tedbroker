How to Choose a Trader

Finding the right traders to copy is crucial for your success. Here's what to look for:

01

Track Record

Look for traders with a consistent performance history over at least 6 months. Past performance isn't a guarantee, but it's a good indicator.

02

Risk Level

Check the trader's risk score and drawdown history. Choose traders whose risk tolerance matches your own investment goals.

03

Trading Style

Understand if they're day traders, swing traders, or long-term investors. Different styles perform better in different market conditions.

04

Diversification

Select traders who focus on different markets and assets to build a well-rounded portfolio that can weather various market conditions.

Risk Management Tools

Our platform provides powerful tools to help you manage risk and protect your investment while copy trading.

Investment Allocation

Control how much of your portfolio is allocated to each trader, allowing you to balance risk across your investments.

Real-time Monitoring

Track the performance of your copied traders in real-time with detailed analytics and notifications for important events.
