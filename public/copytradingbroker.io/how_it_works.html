How Copy Trading Works

A simple yet powerful system that connects investors with expert traders

1. Connection

Our platform connects investors looking for returns with experienced traders seeking additional income. The system automatically matches based on risk profiles and trading styles.

2. Replication

When a trader executes a position, the same trade is automatically replicated in the investor's account, adjusted proportionally based on the investment amount allocated.

3. Profit Sharing

Investors keep the majority of profits generated, while traders earn performance fees based on successful trades. This creates a win-win relationship aligned with mutual success.
