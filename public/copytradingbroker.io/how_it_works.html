Copy Trading Platform

The Easiest Way to Follow Successful Traders and Replicate Their Success

Our copy trading platform connects investors with expert traders, allowing you to automatically copy their trades and benefit from their expertise without having to analyze markets yourself.

Start Trading Today

For Investors: How to Copy Trades

Follow these simple steps to start copying successful traders and potentially grow your investment

1. Create an Account

Sign up for a free account on our platform. Complete your profile and verify your identity to get started.

2. Fund Your Account

Deposit funds into your account using one of our secure payment methods. Start with an amount you're comfortable with.

3. Choose Traders

Browse our marketplace of verified traders. Review their performance history, risk level, and trading style before selecting.

4. Start Copying

Set your copy trading parameters, including investment amount and risk management settings. Then activate copying to start automatically replicating trades.

For Traders: How to Get Followed

Share your trading expertise and earn additional income by allowing others to copy your trades

1. Register as a Trader

Create an account and apply to become a featured trader. Complete your profile with your trading experience and strategy.

2. Connect Trading Account

Link your existing trading account or create a new one through our platform. Set up API connections to share your trades.

3. Build Your Track Record

Trade consistently and build a verifiable performance history. Focus on risk management and consistent returns to attract followers.

4. Earn From Followers

Set your commission structure and start earning from successful trades. Receive performance fees based on profits generated for your followers.

How Copy Trading Works

A simple yet powerful system that connects investors with expert traders

1. Connection

Our platform connects investors looking for returns with experienced traders seeking additional income. The system automatically matches based on risk profiles and trading styles.

2. Replication

When a trader executes a position, the same trade is automatically replicated in the investor's account, adjusted proportionally based on the investment amount allocated.

3. Profit Sharing

Investors keep the majority of profits generated, while traders earn performance fees based on successful trades. This creates a win-win relationship aligned with mutual success.
