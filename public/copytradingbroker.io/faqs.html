TED Brokers is a platform that allows users to mirror the trades of experienced and successful traders in real-time. It’s an excellent way for beginners to participate in financial markets without needing deep trading knowledge. All trading is conducted transparently, and users can view trader performance metrics before selecting someone to follow. Our platform supports a variety of financial instruments including forex, cryptocurrencies, indices, and commodities.

We also offer tools to help you monitor your account performance, risk levels, and profitability so you can make informed decisions. Whether you’re new to trading or simply prefer a more hands-off approach, copy trading provides a smart, convenient entry point into the financial world.

Creating an account with TED Brokers is quick and straightforward. Simply sign up using your email address or social login, and complete your profile with basic information such as your name, contact number, and country of residence. To comply with international regulations and ensure platform security, all users are required to verify their identity.

This involves uploading a valid government-issued ID (such as a passport or driver's license) and a recent utility bill or bank statement to confirm your address. Verification typically takes 1–2 business days, and you will be notified via email once your account is approved. Verified users gain full access to all features including funding, withdrawals, and copy trading.

We support multiple funding options including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and popular e-wallets such as Skrill, Neteller, and cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and USDT. Once your account is verified, you can fund your trading wallet directly from your dashboard. Most deposits are processed instantly, while bank transfers may take 1–3 business days.

Withdrawals are just as simple. Navigate to the withdrawal section, choose your preferred method, and enter the amount. Please note that for security reasons, withdrawals must be made using the same method used for deposit. Our team processes withdrawal requests within 24 hours, though times may vary based on your selected payment provider.

While copy trading can offer a hands-off approach to investing, it's important to understand the associated risks. Past performance of a trader does not guarantee future results, and market conditions can change rapidly. Following a trader with a high risk appetite could result in significant losses, especially if you don't monitor your exposure or adjust your portfolio accordingly.

We encourage users to diversify by following multiple traders with different strategies, use the available risk management tools such as stop-loss limits, and never invest more than you can afford to lose. TED Brokers provides detailed trader analytics and risk scores to help you make informed choices, but ultimately, the responsibility lies with the investor.
